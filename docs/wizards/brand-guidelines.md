# Precise Ventures: Process Wizard — Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Process Wizard  
**Parent Organization:** Precise Ventures  
**Industry:** Engineering and Regulatory Compliance SaaS

**Purpose**  
Process Wizard enables engineers and regulatory specialists to manage, review, and approve procedures with precision and accountability. It simplifies compliance workflows without compromising control or traceability.

**Mission Statement**  
To bring structure, clarity, and confidence to complex regulatory and procedural environments.

**Core Values**
- **Precision:** Every workflow, field, and approval is traceable.
- **Integrity:** Transparent data handling and audit-ready systems.
- **Efficiency:** Designed for speed without sacrificing rigor.
- **Professionalism:** Every interface reflects trust and competence.

**Elevator Pitch**  
Process Wizard is a professional-grade SaaS for engineering and regulatory teams to create, manage, and approve procedures. Built for compliance, designed for usability, and trusted for accuracy.

## 2. Logo System

![Process Wizard Logo](../Logo.jpg)

**Primary Logo**

A clean wordmark: `PROCESS Wizard`
- **Typeface:** Poppins SemiBold (uppercase for “PROCESS,” title case for “Wizard”)
- **Alignment:** Horizontal
- **Tagline (optional):** Professional Compliance, Simplified.

**Symbol / Icon Concept**

A minimalist wand tip forming a check mark inside a circle, symbolizing precision and validation. Use the symbol as a favicon, app icon, or document watermark.

**Color Usage**
- **Primary color background:** white or slate grey (`#F5F6F8` or `#1E1E1E`)
- **Wordmark:** dark blue or near-black (`#1B1F23`)
- **Accent in logo mark:** process blue (`#0070F3`)

**Logo Clear Space**

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter “P” on all sides.

**Minimum Size**
- **Print:** 25 mm wide
- **Screen:** 160 px wide

**Incorrect Uses**
- Do not stretch or rotate.
- Do not add shadows or gradients.
- Do not change typeface or proportions.

## 4. Typography

- **Primary Typeface:** Inter (Google Fonts) — modern, clean, readable. Use for all UI and body text.
- **Secondary Typeface:** Poppins (Google Fonts) — use for headings and the brand name for its geometric precision.
- **Code Typeface:** JetBrains Mono — use in examples, configuration screens, and developer materials.

**Hierarchy**

| Level | Typeface | Weight | Size       | Usage                |
|-------|----------|--------|------------|----------------------|
| H1    | Poppins  | 600    | 36–48 px   | Main headers         |
| H2    | Poppins  | 500    | 24–32 px   | Section titles       |
| H3    | Inter    | 600    | 18–20 px   | Subsection headers   |
| Body  | Inter    | 400    | 16 px      | Standard copy        |
| Small | Inter    | 400    | 13–14 px   | Captions, metadata   |

- **Line spacing:** 1.4× font size
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned, ragged right

## 5. Iconography and Graphics

- **Style:** Line icons with consistent 2 px stroke width.
- **Color:** Use Process Blue (`#0070F3`) or Graphite (`#3C4043`).
- **Corner Radius:** Slight (2–3 px) for harmony with typography.
- **Metaphors:** Flow, structure, connection, compliance.
- Do not use illustrative or cartoon-like icons.

## 6. Layout and Composition

**Grid System**
- 12-column grid for web, 8-column grid for mobile.
- Gutters: 24 px.
- Container width: 1200 px max.

**Spacing Rules**
- Major section spacing: 64 px top and bottom.
- Minor spacing: 16–24 px between cards or text blocks.
- Maintain at least one full grid gutter between adjacent visual elements.

**Use of Whitespace**

Generous whitespace communicates control and professionalism. Avoid visual clutter or unnecessary framing.

## 7. Tone of Voice

**Personality**
- Direct, competent, factual.
- Analytical, not emotional.
- Confident without exaggeration.

**Voice Principles**
- Use precise verbs: “Verify,” “Approve,” “Record,” “Publish.”
- Avoid informal terms like “wizardly,” “magic,” or “quick fix.”
- Maintain grammatical clarity and technical professionalism.

**Examples**
- ✅ “Submit procedure for review.”
- ❌ “Let’s check that off your list!”

- **Sentence Length:** 10–20 words preferred.
- **Perspective:** Active voice, second person (“You can manage…”) or imperative (“Select procedure…”).

## 8. Applications

**Web UI**
- Primary button: Process Blue background, white text.
- Secondary button: white background, Process Blue border.
- Background: light grey (`#F5F6F8`).
- Tables: subtle zebra striping (`#FFFFFF` / `#F9FAFB`).
- Cards: 8 px corner radius, 1 px border (`#E5E7EB`).

**Document Templates**
- Header: Logo left, document title right.
- Footer: Page number + “Process Wizard | © Precise Ventures”.
- Fonts: Inter Regular (body), Poppins SemiBold (titles).

**Default Template Pack by Document Type**
- Intake Form: `intake-default` template + `intake-default-light` stylesheet.
- Clinical Assessment: `assessment-default` template + `assessment-default-light` stylesheet.
- Workout Log: `workout-log-default` template + `workout-log-default-light` stylesheet.
- Training Plan: `training-plan-default` template + `training-plan-default-light` stylesheet.
- Verification Report (PDF proof): `verification-report-default` template + `verification-report-default-light` stylesheet.

Each default template in this pack SHALL include the required header/footer and typography conventions above so generated output remains brand-compliant even before tenant customization.

**Slide Decks**
- Background: white or slate grey.
- Title slides: Process Blue banner across top 15%.
- Font: Poppins for headings, Inter for content.

## 9. Brand Consistency

- Apply colors and fonts only as specified.
- Always maintain clear hierarchy and whitespace.
- Test accessibility and contrast before deployment.
- Use the same language conventions across UI, documentation, and marketing.
